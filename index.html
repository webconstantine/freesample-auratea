Our Story

Passion, Purity & Perfection

Origin

Born in the Highlands of Kenya

High above sea level, where morning mist lingers over rolling green hills, lies the origin of every Aura leaf. Kenya's highlands offer some of the world's most prized tea-growing conditions — rich volcanic soil, consistent rainfall, and cool altitude air that slows the growth of the leaf, concentrating its flavour.

Our premium A-grade leaves are handpicked with care, choosing only the finest two leaves and a bud. They are unblended and entirely free from artificial additives, colouring, or preservatives.

1960
Heritage

Over Six Decades of Craft

Aura Premium Tea began in 1960 with a single, quiet conviction — that the finest tea deserved to be shared honestly, without compromise. What started as a family passion rooted in the love of nature grew, batch by batch, into a name trusted by tea lovers across the world.

Every harvest honours that founding promise. No shortcuts. No blending to mask imperfections. Just the natural strength, rich aroma, and timeless character of leaves that carry the full story of the land in every steep.

Now in Pakistan
Culture

Tea is Pakistan's Language of Connection

In Pakistan, tea is not simply a beverage. It is how mornings begin, how guests are welcomed, how conversations deepen, and how comfort is offered without words. It is woven into the fabric of daily life — from the chai dhaba on a busy corner to the quiet cup shared between family at dusk.

Aura Tea arrives in Pakistan not as a foreign product, but as a kindred spirit. A tea worthy of this culture.
