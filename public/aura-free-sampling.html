Got Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the free sampling campaign and Aura Premium Tea.

How do I request a free sample?

Simply fill out the free sample reservation form on this page. If your area is eligible, our team will confirm your reservation on WhatsApp and send a complimentary sample to your address.

Is the sample really free?

Yes. There is no payment and no card required. The sampling campaign is limited to one free sample per household while stock lasts.

Which areas are eligible?

We are currently sampling in major cities across Pakistan, starting with Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Lahore and Karachi. Submit your address and our team will confirm eligibility for your location.

What makes Aura Premium Tea different?

Aura Premium Tea is made with premium unblended tea leaves sourced from Kenya. Unlike blended teas, every cup delivers a naturally rich taste, strong aroma, and consistent quality.

What does unblended tea mean?

Unblended tea is made from carefully selected tea leaves without mixing different grades or fillers, resulting in a purer, richer, and more authentic tea experience.

Does Aura Tea make strong karak chai?

Yes. Aura is specially selected to deliver a rich, full-bodied karak chai with a strong aroma. It works beautifully for both milk tea and black tea.
